I am Mei Li, a faculty member at the School of Information and Artificial Intelligence (School of Industrial Software), Yangzhou University.

My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and intelligent systems, with a particular focus on:

- Graph Neural Networks (GNN)
- Causal Inference
- Brain Network Modeling
- Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI)
- Internet of Things (IoT)

My current research aims to develop robust and interpretable AI models for complex systems, especially in biomedical data analysis and intelligent IoT applications.

## Academic Activities

- Teaching undergraduate and graduate courses in IoT and AI
- Conducting interdisciplinary research in AI + healthcare
- Publishing in international journals and conferences
